About These Visualizations

These interactive visualizations showcase dive patterns, behaviors, and energy expenditure data from marine mammal tagging. They use Plotly.js to provide full interactivity:

  • Click and drag to rotate 3D visualizations
  • Hover over data points to see detailed information
  • Zoom in/out using mouse wheel or pinch gestures
  • Reset the view using the home button in the toolkit
  • Download as PNG using the camera button

Available Visualizations

Dive Profile with Chronological Coloring

This visualization shows dive depths over time, with colors representing chronological progression from start (purple) to end (yellow). The bottom panel shows dive bouts.

3D Energy Cluster Visualization

This 3D visualization plots energy expenditure against dive depth and energy rate, with colors representing efficiency clusters.

Energy Efficiency Dashboard

This dashboard combines multiple visualizations related to energy efficiency, including cluster distributions, energy by dive phase, and efficiency metrics.
